Motor Royale is an ongoing personal project of mine. I created it for my final year project at university and I am continueing to add to it as I get more skilled at creating games! I have a lot of ideas and a great vision for this game and I hope to be able to create my dream game! It uses a low poly, minimalistic art style and mimics the looks of older arcade style games with improved physics and post processing. I wanted this game to merge the old and new style of games design together! This game is not only a project I am motivated to work on, it is also teaching me a lot about the creation of games!

My favourite achievement was this customization system. In testing I felt the game was quite flat in terms of different cars personalities. I therefore wanted to add a system where the player can change their cars aesthetics. It makes the game more personal and also more enjoyable when the player can see their own custom car being drove around the arena. Considering this was my first time creating this type of system I am very happy with how it turned out.
